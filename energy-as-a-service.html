Energy as a Service (EaaS)

Energy as a Service (EaaS)

We have a vision to create an ecosystem for Electric Vehicle charging stations where people and companies can provide services like parking, supplying energy at their outlets, information about the best routes and much more to make EV charging a simpler process.

Key Features

Empowering sustainable mobility, our values drive innovation, reliability, and accessibility in electric vehicle charging, fostering a cleaner, connected future.

Energy Supply

ChargeZone ensures a reliable Energy Supply, providing electric power to charge electric vehicles swiftly and efficiently. With ChargeZone, drivers can rest assured that their vehicles will be powered up and ready to go whenever they need them.

Parking Services

ChargeZone provides convenient Parking Services, offering designated spots for EVs while they undergo charging. This ensures that drivers can easily find parking and charge their vehicles simultaneously, without the hassle of searching for a charging spot.

Information Services

ChargeZone gives real-time updates on charging availability, prices, and other helpful details, helping users plan their charging stops easily.

Application

ChargeZone has easy-to-use mobile apps that let EV owners find nearby stations, start charging, check on their charging progress, and access extra features, making the charging experience smooth and hassle-free.

Charging Management System

powered by IOT, through the use of Cloud based solutions
One central platform to manage an individual or group of Charge Spot/s. Provides core functionality of Charging Station management, User management, Station management, Business intelligence, Dashboard, MIS Reports. For various business stakeholders, access rights can be configured with respect to administration functions & database information.

Frequently asked questions

What is Energy as a Service(EaaS)?

Energy as a Service (EaaS) is like having a friend take care of everything when you drive an electric vehicle (EV). Imagine you pull into a charging station, and not only can you charge your EV, but you can also find a parking spot easily, get information about the best routes, and have a reliable energy supply. EaaS makes the whole process of charging your EV simple and convenient.

How do I locate your charging stations?

You can use the ChargeZone website or mobile app to find a charging station. These platforms offer a map with every charging station in the area, along with information on the kinds of chargers that are available and their current status.

What types of charging connectors do you support?

ChargeZone is compatible with multiple charging connector types, such as Type 2 (IEC 62196) for AC charging and CCS (Combined Charging System) for fast charging of both AC and DC. ChargeZone also provides extremely quick charging stations with powerful CCS connections, allowing for quick charging of compatible electric vehicles and a significant reduction in charging time. Thanks to this, EV users can be assured of wide compatibility and convenience.

What charging speeds are available?

ChargeZone offers multiple charging speeds, including standard AC charging (7-22 kW) for overnight charging in 4-8 hours, fast DC charging (50-150 kW) for 80% charge in 30-60 minutes, and super-fast DC charging (150-350 kW) for 80% charge in just 15-30 minutes. This ensures flexibility and convenience for all EV users.
